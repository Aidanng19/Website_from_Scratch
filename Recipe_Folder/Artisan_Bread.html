Artisan Bread

Plain bread recipe

Ingredients

  1. 3.25 cups All-Purpose Flour
  2. 2.25 tsp Yeast
  3. 2 tsp Kosher Salt
  4. 1.5 cups Warm Water
  5. 3 tlbs butter

Optional

  1. 1 tsp garlic powder

Post-proof additives

  1. 1/4 cup chocolate chunks
  2. Apple slices
  3. 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Recipe

  1. 1. In a large bowl, mix the ingredients (with garlic powder) with a large wooden spoon until the dough is formed. Beat a butter and mix it in.
  2. 2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and allow the dough to rise for 8-24 hours in the refridgerator or in a cold environment at less than 100F.
  3. 3. Remove the dough from the refridgerator and allow it to warm up to room temperature.
  4. 4. Flour the surface of the dough and fold it on itself a few times before forming it into your desired shape.
  5. 5. Optional: Mix in your post-proof additives while folding it to incorporate your ingredients.
  6. 6. Cut long slashes on the surface to make it look cool and to allow the bread to rise more evenly.
  7. 7. Bake the bread at 425F for 18 minutes.
  8. 8. Let the bread rest for 20 minutes before cutting it open.

Notes

11/24/2025: I added sugar to my bread and it definitely added a little extra flavor that I thought my last batch was missing. I didn't like the sugar aftertaste, but I think that might just be me because my coworkers liked it. I like the current recipe and I'll add chocolate next time and see how that ends up.
