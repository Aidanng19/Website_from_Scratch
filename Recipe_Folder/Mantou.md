## Ingredients
2 cups all-purpose flour\
1.5 tbsp sugar\
0.5 cup warm water\
1 tsp yeast\
2 tsp lard/oil\

## Instructions
1. Dissolve sugar and yeast in warm water. Mix, then let sit for 10 minutes.
2. Add flour, lard and mix until dough is formed. 
3. Let the dough proof for a few hours.
4. Cut up the dough into knuckle sized chunks. 
5. Place them on parchment paper and let it rest for 30 minutes.
6. Steam them on high heat for 15 minutes.
