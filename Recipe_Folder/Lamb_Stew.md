## Ingredients
Lamb Chunks\
Carrots\
Potatoes\
Onions\
Celery

# Dry Seasonings
Salt\
Black Pepper\
Bay Leaves\
Italian Seasoning\
Ginger\
Garlic

# Wet Seasonings
Chicken broth\
Worcestershire Sauce\
Olive Oil

## Instructions
1. Oil the bottom of the instant pot with olive oil. Put the ingredients in and saute on high with dry seasonings to force the flavor into the ingredients.
2. Pour in chicken broth and worcestershire sauce until it almost covers the ingredients.
3. Pressure cook for 15 minutes and naturally release.
