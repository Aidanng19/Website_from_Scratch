## Ingredients
# Roux ingredients
3 tbsp All-Purpose Flour\
1/4 cup milk\
1/4 cup water

# Dough ingredients
3.25 cups All-Purpose Flour\
2.25 tsp Yeast\
2 tsp Kosher Salt\
1/4 cup sugar\
1 egg\
1.25 cup room temp milk\
4 tbsp softened butter

## Instructions
# Making the roux first
1. Slowly heat the roux ingredients in a small pot on low heat until they are all mixed. The roux should be thickened, but pourable.

# Making the dough
1. Start by mixing the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
2. Add the egg, milk, and roux once the powder is well mixed and kneed for 10 minutes until the dough is formed.

# Making the topping
1. There are different ingredients for this. Lets start with the dough first. 

Bake at 350F for 35-40 minutes.
